About the Trip

After I finished High School, my brother and I decided to go travelling somewhere and since I was the one graduating, I got to decide where to go. I wanted to go to Italy because it's such a diverse country with so much to see. Things like ancient architecture and history as well as the mountains, art, and food. Especially the food. I can neither confirm nor deny any idea that I only went to Italy just because I wanted to try real Italian pizza 🍕.
